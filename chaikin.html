Chaikin Visualizer

Made by Carl Holmqvist, 2024

Welcome to a Chaikin's corner cutting algorithm visualizer!

Click inside the bordered area to place points, as many as you like!
You can also move individual points by clicking + holding a point and then dragging it.
Click the "+" or "-" buttons, or change drag the slider to increase/decrease the number of iterations the corner cutting algorithm runs, and see the changes in the resulting curve.
